About Us

Our Story

Meet Thip, the skilled beautician behind Beauty Royal House. Since establishing her home-based salon in Ross-on-Wye, she has created a welcoming space where professional beauty care meets personal attention. With over ten years of expertise and eight professional certifications, Thip brings dedication and skill to every treatment.

As a single mother of two, she understands the importance of flexible, accessible beauty care. This inspired her to create a salon that offers both convenience and quality, complete with free parking and ground-floor access. Her attention to detail and commitment to client comfort have made Beauty Royal House a trusted name in local beauty care.

Originally from Kalasin, Thailand, Thip moved to the UK in 2010, bringing with her a strong work ethic and vision for client-focused beauty services.
